The Brutalist Web Manifesto

Raw Materials Only

No gradients. No shadows. No rounded corners. Pure geometric forms expressing their function without apology.

Honest Typography

Monospace fonts that reveal the code beneath. No smooth curves hiding the digital truth of pixels and vectors.

Exposed Structure

Show the grid. Reveal the container. Let users see the scaffolding that supports their experience.

Unforgiving Hierarchy

Important information dominates through size and weight, not through subtle color variations or gentle spacing.

Binary Color Palette

Black, white, and warning red. Three colors are sufficient to communicate any interface.

Anti-Animation

Smooth transitions mask the digital nature of interfaces. Changes should be immediate and honest.
